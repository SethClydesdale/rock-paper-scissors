How to play

Rock-paper-scissors or "Roshambo" is a game that can be played with two or more players. In this version you'll be facing JavaScript itself. Can you defeat this almighty object-oriented programming language ? The rules and gameplay will be detailed below.

You can choose from three hand gestures ; Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock. If an equal gesture is drawn it will result in a tie. Study the diagram below long and hard to become a Roshambo master.


Diagram courtesy of Wikipedia.

Below is an overview of the game screen with a brief explanation of the User Interface. You choose your hand gesture or "weapon" with the three buttons in the top left hand corner. Once you made your choice click the "GO" button to begin the battle with the CPU. Note that you can still change your choice after hitting the "GO" button. Make use of this if you're unsure of the choice you made !!


Click the image to see the full size.

You will be notified of the results at the end of the round. If there's a winner, one point will be added to their score count. Now get out there and beat that CPU !
